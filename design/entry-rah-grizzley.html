    Rah Grizzley

    Rapper · MC

    Hip-hop Underground hip-hop

    Rah Grizzley, who records as Rah Grizz, is a hardcore underground hip hop lyricist born in Flatbush, Brooklyn, who has spent years living in and representing Jersey City.

    In the Record§

    He returned to recording after a break of roughly six years away from the scene.

    His catalog includes “That Talk” and “Aggression,” the latter featuring the late Brooklyn MC Sean Price of Heltah Skeltah and the Boot Camp Clik.
