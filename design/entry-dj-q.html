    DJ Q

    DJ · c. late 1990s – 2000s

    Hip-hop Mixtapes c. late 1990s – 2000s

    DJ Q — billed on records and flyers as DJ "Q" and known today as Da Topic — is a Jersey City mixtape and party DJ who came up through the city's late-1990s and 2000s club, mixtape, and battle circuit.

    In the Record§

    He was part of the Stan's Square Records mixtape generation, sharing bills and battle cards with peers including DJ 007, DJ Nel E Nel, DJ Dyce, DJ E Double, and DJ P Dub.

    Flyers from the period place him across the era's marquee events — battling live at the 1st Annual Winter Blaze mixtape DJ competition in 2000, appearing on Laydown-N-Die Records' "Dream Team" poster, and billed at Summer Heatwave 2002 among "Jersey City's Finest."

    He held club nights across the city and neighboring Newark — from Hipnotiq Thursdays at Foxes on St. Paul's Avenue to Stand Up Fridays at the Paradise Club on Broadway — and was among the invited DJs for Rap Wars Round 2 in 2003.

    He is named in both DJ DX's and DJ 007's segments of the 2006 Jersey City DJ Documentary; his identity was confirmed by DJ DX in 2026.
