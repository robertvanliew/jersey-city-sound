    Entry №. 011

    DJ Flash

    DJ · Mixtape Pioneer

    Jersey City DJ from Park Street, widely credited as the originator of the city's mixtape scene. Began DJing at age seven, practiced all five elements of the craft, and built beats on two turntables and two tape decks before samplers existed.

    Entry №. 004

    Wiztv

    DJ · Videographer · Documentarian

    Formerly billed as DJ Wizard, Wiztv directed the nearly three-hour Jersey City DJ Documentary Vol. 1 (2006) and operates the video platforms that preserve the scene's parties, battles, and music videos to this day.

    Entry №. 001

    DJ DX

    DJ · Producer · Turntablist

    Robert Van Liew, known professionally as DJ DX, is a Jersey City DJ and producer active since the late 1990s — named after the Double X Posse, schooled on the MPC by Mark Cee, and documented live in the 2006 documentary.
