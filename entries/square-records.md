## Career

- Square Records is a Jersey City record label founded by Stan Krause, associated with his Stan's Square Records store and his Catamount Records label
- The label released Chill Divine's 1990 hip-hop 12-inch, “Puttin' It Wildly / Long Live the Lyrical,” connecting the Krause record-store lineage to the city's early hip-hop era
