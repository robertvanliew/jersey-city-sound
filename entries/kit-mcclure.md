## Career

- Kit McClure (born 1951) is a jazz musician, bandleader of the all-female big band Kit McClure Band and founder of the Women in Jazz Project.
- In 2004, she started a project to revive interest in the all-female big band, International Sweethearts of Rhythm.
- She has been a featured player with the Barry White Orchestra and toured with Sam & Dave.
