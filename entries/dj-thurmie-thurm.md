## Career

- DJ Thurmie Thurm, also known as DJ Thurm, is a battle DJ and pioneer of the original Jersey City hip-hop and house scene, best known for his late-1970s and early-1980s work as a founding member of the Jersey City Chiba Crew
- He came up in the “Chilltown” scene playing park jams, community centers, and local spots, in an era alongside local legends including Grand Wizard Capone, Nicky Barnes, and the Sweet, Slick & Sly Crew
- Decades on, he remains a respected figure in Chilltown history, celebrated across East Coast music-history circles
