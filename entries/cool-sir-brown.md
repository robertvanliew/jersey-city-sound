## Career

- Cool Sir Brown is a Jersey City hip-hop artist known for classic and modern rap tracks, including the single “Sir Brown Lives”
- He has drawn attention for collaborations with the late hip-hop legend Biz Markie, and released the project Sir Brown Lives
- He is the brother of two-time Grammy-nominated songwriter Mary Brown and a grandson of Kathryn Van Liew — a nephew of soul singer McArthur Munford and part of the four-cornerstone Van Liew musical dynasty alongside Chill Divine (Gary Van Liew) and DJ DX (Robert Van Liew)
