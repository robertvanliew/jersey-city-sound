## Career

- Christopher Charles O'Neal, formerly known as MaLus the PoeT and better known by his stage name Chris Phoenix, is an American rapper and producer.
- Along with his partner Marcus Sibley, better known as Walkin Contradiction, he is the co-founder of the independent record label Beyond Musiq Records (BMR).
- He is the host of the Urban Juke Joint, a well-known poetry event held at the Baháʼí Center in NYC.
