## Career

- Randy Nicholls, known as Ransom, is a Jersey City rapper who moved to the city around age eight
- One half of the A-Team with Hitchcock; the duo were affiliates of DJ Clue's Desert Storm alongside Fabolous and Stack Bundles
- The A-Team released the Hardhood Classics mixtape trilogy, building a reputation for punchlines and grimy verses on the New Jersey mixtape circuit
- After a Def Jam deal did not materialize the A-Team split around 2006; Ransom has continued a prolific solo catalog
