## Career

- Chill Rob G (born Robert Frazier) is an influential Jersey City rapper and a pioneering voice of hip-hop's Golden Era
- He rose to prominence in the late 1980s as an original member of DJ Mark the 45 King's legendary Flavor Unit collective, alongside Queen Latifah, Apache, and Lakim Shabazz
- His 1989 debut Ride the Rhythm — featuring “Court Is Now in Session” and “Dope Rhymes” — is a critically acclaimed classic of the era
- He returned with Empires Crumble (2022), a boom-bap comeback on Chuck D's SpitSLAM Label Group featuring a Flavor Unit reunion, and Survival of the Better (2026), executive produced by Chuck D and produced entirely by C-Doc
