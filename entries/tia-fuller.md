## Career

- Tia Fuller (born March 27, 1976) is an American saxophonist, composer, and educator, and a member of the all-female band touring with Beyoncé.
- Fuller is currently a faculty member in the ensembles department at Berklee College of Music.
- Fuller was a Featured Jazz Musician in Pixar's animated film Soul.
