## Career

- Rapper and media personality raised in Jersey City from age 13; he rose from the mixtape circuit to national success
- His single 'Pump It Up' (2003) reached No. 38 on the Billboard Hot 100 and earned a Grammy nomination for Best Male Rap Solo Performance
- Founding member of the rap supergroup Slaughterhouse, which signed to Eminem's Shady Records and released two studio albums
- Host of The Joe Budden Podcast (2015–present), one of hip-hop's most successful independent podcasts, now past 900 episodes
