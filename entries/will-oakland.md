## Career

- Will Oakland (January 15, 1880 – May 15, 1956) was an American countertenor famed for his exceptionally high vocal range.
- He was born Herman Hinrichs in Jersey City, New Jersey, to German-American immigrant parents.
- Oakland began his musical career after leaving the United States Army in 1905, joining Lew Dockstader's minstrels in Rochester, New York.
