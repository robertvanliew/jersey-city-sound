## Career

- DJ Stranger Dee is a foundational figure of the Jersey City hip-hop and DJ scene, who gained local fame in the early 1980s as a pioneer for the Sweet, Slick and Sly Crew
- With his crew he performed at seminal events including the New Jersey vs. Bronx showdowns at the historic Benmore Skating Rink in Jersey City
- He worked as a DJ on major hip-hop tours alongside groups like Public Enemy in the late 1980s, and is respected as a founding father of the Jersey City (“Chilltown”) music movement
