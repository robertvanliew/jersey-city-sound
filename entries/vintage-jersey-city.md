## Career

- Vintage is a Jersey City a cappella group formed by McArthur Munford of the Spellbinders with Ray Block of the Valitons
- The group's recordings were issued through Stan Krause's Catamount Records; the ensemble later continued as New Vintage and remained active in the New York area
