## Career

- Fit for an Autopsy is an American deathcore band from Jersey City, New Jersey, formed in 2008.
- The band consists of guitarists Will Putney, Pat Sheridan and Tim Howley, drummer Josean Orta, lead vocalist Joe Badolato, and bassist Peter "Blue" Spinazola.
- They are currently signed to Nuclear Blast and have released seven studio albums since formation.
