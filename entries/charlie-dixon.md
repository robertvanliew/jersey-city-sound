## Career

- Charles Edward Dixon (December 31, 1898 in Jersey City, New Jersey – December 6, 1940 in New York City) was an American jazz banjoist.
- Between 1921 and 1923, Dixon was a member of Johnny Dunn's Original Jazz Hounds, one of several Dunn-led line-ups that recorded in New York around that time for the Columbia label.
- Dixon played in local ensembles in Boston and New York before becoming a member of Sam Wooding's orchestra in 1922.
