## Career

- Maude Roberts George (September 27, 1888 – December 1, 1943) was an American singer, arts administrator, and music critic.
- She was president of the National Association of Negro Musicians (NANM) from 1933 to 1935.
- She was also president of the Chicago Music Association, and a music critic for The Chicago Defender newspaper.
