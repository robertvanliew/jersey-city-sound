## Career

- Albert Laurence Di Meola (born July 22, 1954) is an American guitarist.
- Known for his work in jazz fusion and world music, he had his breakthrough after joining Chick Corea's Return to Forever group in 1974.
- He launched, from 1976 afterwards, a successful and critically acclaimed solo career, noted for his technical mastery, complex compositions and explorations of Latin music.
