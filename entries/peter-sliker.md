## Career

- Peter Sliker (June 7, 1925 – June 25, 2010) was an American operatic bass-baritone who had a lengthy career performing at the Metropolitan Opera from 1961 through 1989.
- He specialized in portraying comprimario roles and was admired for his comic timing.
- He has appeared on several Live from the Metropolitan Opera broadcasts and Metropolitan Opera radio broadcasts.
