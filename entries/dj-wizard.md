## Career

- DJ Wizard, also known as WizTV, is a Jersey City DJ, videographer, and documentarian
- Directed and produced 'Wiztv Presents: The Jersey City DJ Documentary Vol. 1' (2006), a nearly three-hour document of Jersey City's mixtape DJ scene
- Producer of the Jersey Club Blends mixtape series (Vol. 3, 2017) and blends with DJ Sliink ('Not Gunna Get Us')
- Operates the WizTV video platforms (youtube.com/wiztvhs, youtube.com/WiztvPartyVideos) documenting the scene's parties and music videos, including videos featuring DJ DX
- As of 2026, producing a new WizTV Jersey Club Documentary (Part 1 previewed via Patreon/Instagram)
