## Career

- Directed by DJ Wizard (WizTV); a nearly three-hour (2:57:58) documentary of Jersey City's mixtape DJ scene
- Features interviews and home-studio performances from DJ Flash, Mista Quietman, Mark Cee, DJ 007, Man Money, DJ E Double (Double Platinum Entertainment), Big Time, DJ Dolo, DJ DX, X5, Chameleon (Ka-Million), DJ Simmons, DJ Madden, Benito, Joe Bananas, cover designer Blaze, and others
- Includes bonus footage from the Winterblaze mixtape battle held at the Boys Club (2000), with battle organizer Sean interviewed and DJ Savage among competitors
- Documents Jersey City style markers: blend-driven mixtapes, the '201 style' of doubled drums, and stacked-acapella blends
- Preserves oral history including turntable battles at Audubon Park (late 1970s–early 1980s) where the winner took the loser's equipment
- Documents scene venues and institutions: Rendezvous (formerly Ruthie's), the Boys Club, Lollipop, Taste, and Ferris High School as a producer of scene legends
