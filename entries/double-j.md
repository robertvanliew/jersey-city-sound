## Career

- Double J (Gerald “J” Jones), known as “The Hitman Double J,” is a foundational Jersey City hip-hop artist who helped pioneer the East Coast rap scene in the late 1980s and early 1990s as a core member of Queen Latifah's original Flavor Unit
- Growing up in Jersey City he hosted block parties alongside future stars like Apache; in 1988 he joined DJ Mark the 45 King's Flavor Unit
- He appears on the 1990 posse cut “Flavor Unit Assassination Squad,” released the 12-inch “Cannibal Town / Def Style” and the album The Hitman, and is known for heavy bass lines on tracks like “Double J'z Tantrum”
- He later resurfaced as half of the Maniac Mob, with DJ Mark the 45 King resuming production on several independent releases
