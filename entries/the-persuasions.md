## Career

- The Persuasions are an American a cappella group long celebrated as “the kings of a cappella,” whose early career ran through Jersey City
- Frank Zappa first heard the group singing over the phone from Stan Krause's Stan's Square Records store; Krause managed the group and issued their early recordings on his Catamount Records label
- The group went on to a decades-long recording career across major and independent labels, influencing generations of a cappella and vocal-harmony singers
