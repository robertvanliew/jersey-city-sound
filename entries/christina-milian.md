## Career

- Christina Milian ( MIL-ee-AHN, née Flores; born September 26, 1981) is an American singer, songwriter, and actress.
- Following a number of minor television and film roles in the late 1990s, Milian made her recording debut on American rapper Ja Rule's 2000 single "Between Me and You", which peaked at number 11 on the Billboard Hot 100.
- Under Island Records, she released her 2001 debut single "AM to PM", which peaked within the top 40 of the Billboard Hot 100.
