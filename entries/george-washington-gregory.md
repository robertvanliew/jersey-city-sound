## Career

- George Washington Gregory (February 15, 1867 – May 2, 1902) was a banjo player, playing in the classic banjo style.
- A composer, he arranged classical music for the 5-string banjo.
- He taught the banjo as a music teacher and wrote a musical method, Practical Fingering for the Banjo, originally published in serial.
