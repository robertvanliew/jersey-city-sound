## Career

- DJ E Double, also known as DJ E-Dub DaGeneral (Eric Pinkney), is a Jersey City DJ celebrated as the “Prince of Jersey” — one of the city's most prominent mixtape and club DJs of the late 1990s and early 2000s
- Operating out of Jersey City's 201, E-Dub grew his brand alongside the rise of New Jersey's mixtape and blend culture, and is also billed as E Platinum and “da General”
- In 1995 he founded his entertainment brand, DpEnt. (Double Platinum Entertainment) — the foundation for his promotions, party hosting, and mixtape distribution across the East Coast
- Through the early 2000s he became widely recognized for the signature blends, club mixes, and custom tracks that defined North Jersey's urban nightlife, holding residencies at Rendezvous (formerly Ruthie's), Lollipop, Taste, Brenda's, and Bills
- He is interviewed in the 2006 documentary, where he shouts out the Killa K family; his daughter performs as DJ She Dog
