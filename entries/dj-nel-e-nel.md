## Career

- DJ NEL E NEL, also known as Mandela, is a Jersey City blends mixtape legend, DJ, and producer, also billed as Nel El Blends
- He is credited with production and blends for artists including Joe Budden, Ransom, PnB Rock, and N.O.R.E.

- He is said to have put out a Notorious B.I.G. album ahead of its official release — a claim held in the scene's oral history
- By community account, he won the Winter Blaze 2000 DJ battle held at the Jersey City Boys & Girls Club
- He sold mixtapes through Stan's Square Records alongside the city's top mixtape DJs
