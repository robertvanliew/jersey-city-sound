### №011 — DJ Flash  `doc-verified`
*DJ, Mixtape pioneer · c. 1970s–present*
- Jersey City DJ from Park Street, widely credited in the 2006 documentary as the originator of the city's mixtape scene; introduced on camera as a legend
- Began DJing at age seven; describes learning by convincing older neighborhood DJs (including figures like Stranger D) to let him spin
- Practiced all five elements of DJing (beat juggling, scratching, mixing) and describes pre-sampler production using two turntables and two tape decks
- Was part of a five-DJ juggle-battle circle from his block; credits peers including Jamaican Jeff and E Rock as early inspiration
- Names Wimpy B as his 'big brother' in the craft and credits a generation including Stranger D, Fresh Jeff, and Storming Norman (later affiliated with Wu-Tang's circle)
- Operates a store in Jersey City (referenced on camera); mentions 271 Ocean Avenue
- Stepped back from full-time DJing to raise his children, while remaining active
- IG: https://www.instagram.com/djflashhamilton/
- Sources: WizTV Presents: The Jersey City DJ Documentary Vol. 1 (2006)
